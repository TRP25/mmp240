The Best Chocolate Chips Cookies

This is the best chocolate chip cookie recipe ever! Simple ingedients, quick to prepare and amazingly delicious. These chocolate chip cookies turn out perfectly every single time!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter,softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cuo chopped walnuts

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream the butter and sugar together.
  3. Add flour and baking soda, then mix well.
  4. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown.
